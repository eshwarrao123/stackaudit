## FAQ

**How accurate are the savings estimates?**
Our engine is deterministic. We use real-time pricing data from official vendor pages (ChatGPT, Cursor, Claude, etc.) to ensure your waste estimate is 100% accurate, not a guess.

**Do you need access to my bank account?**
No. StackAudit is a privacy-first tool. You manually select your tools and team size. We don't require API access to your financial data for the initial audit.

**What tools do you support?**
We support all major AI tools including ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Runway, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Notion AI, and Perplexity, with new rules added weekly.

**What is the "Optimization Score"?**
It's a 0-100 score that reflects your stack's efficiency. A 100 means you have zero redundant tools and perfect seat utilization. Most startups start at a 65.

**Can I share my report with my team?**
Yes. Every audit generates a unique, shareable link that you can send to your founder or finance lead to justify subscription consolidation.
