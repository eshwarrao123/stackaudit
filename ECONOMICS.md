# Unit Economics & ARR Projections

StackAudit operates on a **Lead-Gen to Consultation** model, targeting a high-intent segment of the B2B SaaS market.

## Conversion Funnel Assumptions
- **Traffic to Audit Completion**: 15% (High intent due to specific search/outreach).
- **Audit Completion to Lead Capture (Email)**: 40% (Users want alerts for new waste).
- **Lead to Bespoke Audit (Paid)**: 5% (Startups over 20 people want a professional to handle the cleanup).

## Unit Economics (Per Lead)
- **Blended CAC**: $12.00 (Mix of cold outreach time and small ad spend).
- **Lead Value (LTV)**: $85.00
  - *Calculation*: 5% conversion to a $1,500 "SaaS Cleanup Service" fee + $200 referral credit from SaaS vendors.

## Profitability Threshold
To reach a "Default Alive" state (covering $5k/month in operating costs):
- Requires ~60 leads per month.
- Requires ~400 completed audits per month.
- **Current Margin**: 85% (Mainly API and hosting costs).

## $1M ARR Scenario Breakdown
To reach $83,333 Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):

| Metric | Target |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Bespoke Audits / Month** | 40 ($2,000 avg fee) |
| **Referral Commissions** | $3,333 / Month |
| **Required Traffic** | 15,000 Monthly Visitors |
| **Required Leads** | 900 Monthly |

### Strategy to $1M
1. **Tier 2 Monetization**: Introduce a $49/month "Continuous Monitor" subscription that syncs with Mercury/Brex API.
2. **Channel Partnerships**: Partner with early-stage VC firms to offer StackAudit as a standard "Day 1" onboarding tool for their portfolio companies, drastically lowering CAC.
3. **Vendor Arbitrage**: Negotiate custom "Move-In" discounts with SaaS vendors (e.g., Cursor, Notion) to offer users who consolidate their stack via our links.
