# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target User & Operational Pain Point
**Target User**: Operations Leads and Founders of AI-heavy startups (Seed to Series A) with teams of 5–30 people.
**Pain Point**: "Subscription Shadow Spend." As teams scale, engineers and marketers often buy their own Pro accounts for Cursor, Midjourney, and Claude. Founders lose track of how many $20/month charges are hitting the company card, and whether they are paying for 10 seats of a tool only 2 people use.

## What users search for before StackAudit
- "Cursor vs Copilot for team"
- "How to cancel Claude Pro team"
- "Startup SaaS spend benchmarks 2026"
- "Reducing AI tool costs"

## Acquisition Channels
1. **Founder Communities**: Direct outreach in "Launch" and "Growth" channels on YC Bookface, South Park Commons, and specialized SaaS Slack groups.
2. **Hacker News (Show HN)**: A technical audience that appreciates the "deterministic" and "privacy-focused" nature of the audit.
3. **Niche Newsletters**: Sponsorships in newsletters like *Ben's Bites* or *TLDR*, targeting people actively looking for new AI tools but wary of spend.

## Unfair Distribution: The Shareable Audit
The "Shareable Report" is the primary growth flywheel. When an Ops lead generates a report showing $400/month in waste, they send the link to the Founder to justify a "SaaS Cleanup Day." The Founder, seeing the clean UI and clear savings, is likely to share the tool with other founders in their network.

## 30-Day Acquisition Plan
- **Week 1 (Cold Outreach)**: DM 50 founders on LinkedIn/Twitter who have recently posted about their AI stack. Goal: 10 completed audits. **Target Traction: 10 users.**
- **Week 2 (The Lead Magnet)**: Launch a "SaaS Waste Calculator" simple tool on Product Hunt. Collect emails for the full StackAudit. **Target Traction: 40 users.**
- **Week 3 (Community Deep-Dive)**: Post detailed "SaaS Overlap" case studies on Reddit (r/startups) and Indie Hackers. **Target Traction: 80 users.**
- **Week 4 (Flywheel)**: Incentivize users to share their "Optimization Score" on Twitter for a free "Bespoke SaaS Consultation." **Target Traction: 150 users.**

## Realistic Expectations
- **Week-1 Traction**: 12 completions (mostly from manual outreach).
- **Conversion**: 15% of visitors complete the audit; 30% of those provide an email for the "Optimization Alerts."
